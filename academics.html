Our curriculum blends national standards with innovative teaching methods to prepare students for Uganda's dynamic future.

Uganda's New Competency-Based Curriculum

We are at the forefront of implementing Uganda's transformative competency-based curriculum, designed to develop practical skills alongside academic knowledge. This national initiative focuses on:

  • Critical thinking - Developing problem-solving abilities relevant to Ugandan contexts
  • Practical skills - Hands-on learning that prepares students for real-world challenges
  • Values education - Instilling ethical principles and national pride
  • Digital literacy - Essential 21st century skills for Uganda's growing tech sector
  • Entrepreneurship - Fostering innovation for Uganda's economic development

Program Structure

Our program strictly follows the Ugandan education system with enhanced learning approaches:

We offer all 12 compulsory subjects as mandated by the Uganda National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC):

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • History
  • Geography
  • Entrepreneurship Education
  • Religious Education (Christian or Islamic)
  • Local Language (Runyankole-Rukiga)
  • Physical Education
  • Information and Communication Technology

Plus optional subjects:

  • French
  • Literature in English
  • Art and Design
  • Agriculture

Students specialize in one of three tracks with subjects approved by Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB):

Science Track

  • Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, ICT
  • Mathematics, Physics, Geography, ICT
  • Biology, Chemistry, Food and Nutrition, Agriculture

Arts/Humanities Track

  • History, Geography, Economics, Literature in English
  • History, Economics, Entrepreneurship, ICT
  • Geography, Economics, Mathematics, Entrepreneurship
  • Religious Studies, Literature in English, History, Economics

Vocational Track

  • Agriculture, Entrepreneurship, ICT, Food and Nutrition
  • Art and Design, Entrepreneurship, ICT, Literature in English

Our Academic Departments

Bweranyangi Girls' offers comprehensive programs through these dedicated departments:

Our science program produces top performers in national exams with modern laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Students participate in national science competitions and practical field studies.

Subjects Offered: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Agriculture, ICT

Mathematics forms the foundation of logical thinking. Our specialized instructors teach both O-Level and A-Level mathematics using interactive methods that make abstract concepts tangible.

Subjects Offered: Mathematics (Pure, Applied), Additional Mathematics

Exploring Uganda's rich heritage and global perspectives through History, Geography, and Entrepreneurship Education - subjects crucial for understanding our nation's development.

Subjects Offered: History, Geography, Entrepreneurship, Economics, Religious Education

Mastery of English (Uganda's official language) plus French and local languages prepares students for regional opportunities and cultural exchange.

Subjects Offered: English Language, Literature in English, French, Runyankole-Rukiga

Enrichment Programs

Beyond the standard curriculum, we offer programs that develop well-rounded individuals:

  • Science Practical Enhancement: Regular laboratory sessions and field trips to apply theoretical knowledge
  • Creative Arts: Music, dance, and drama programs celebrating Ugandan culture
  • Leadership Development: Student council and mentorship programs building tomorrow's female leaders
  • Agricultural Studies: Hands-on farming education relevant to Uganda's economy

Career Preparation

We guide students in making informed decisions about their futures:

  • Orientation Programs: Helping new students (S1 and S5) transition successfully
  • University Preparation: Workshops with representatives from Makerere, Kyambogo, and other Ugandan universities
  • Vocational Guidance: Exploring diverse career paths in Uganda's growing economy
  • Subject Selection Counseling: Assisting S2 students in choosing appropriate S3 subject combinations
